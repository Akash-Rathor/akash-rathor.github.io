Child Safety Standards for AhDares App

Effective date: November 2025

Prohibition of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation

AhDares strictly prohibits any form of child sexual abuse, exploitation, or endangerment on our platform. Content or activity involving harm to children is not tolerated and will result in immediate action, including removal and reporting to relevant authorities.

Handling and Reporting Child Safety Concerns

If you encounter content or behavior that may endanger children, please report it immediately via the in-app feedback system or by contacting the React developer at your-personal-email@example.com. Reports are reviewed promptly and appropriate action is taken in accordance with legal requirements and best practices.

Compliance with Laws

AhDares complies with all applicable child protection laws and regulations, including COPPA and GDPR-K, to ensure a safe environment. Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Point of Contact

For any child safety concerns or questions, please contact the React developer at your-personal-email@example.com.

Continuous Review

We regularly review our safety and reporting policies to ensure maximum protection for our users and full compliance with Google Play's Child Safety Standards.
